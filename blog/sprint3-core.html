Emotional Intelligence

To put it simply, Emotional Intelligence is the ability to be aware of emotions, and control them. It also involves good motivation, empathy and social skills.

This differs to IQ, which is more about the ability to gain knowledge, and use that knowledge to solve problems. IQ is mainly about problem solving, wheras EQ is more about mental stability. Together they make up most of the skills that are sought after in any industry.

The three main benefits of having good EQ are high performance, leadership and happiness.
